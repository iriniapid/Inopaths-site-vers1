About The Interactive Decarbonisation Simulator

The Interactive Decarbonisation Simulator (IDS) is a comprehensive, fully interactive energy model that is used by policy makers, industry associates, researchers and the general public across Europe. It is used to design decarbonisation strategies, evaluate energy scenarios and investigate the synergies and trade-offs in different low-carbon transitions.
